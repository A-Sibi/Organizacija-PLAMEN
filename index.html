Organizacija "PLAMEN" is a dedicated NGO focused on advancing education, sports, culture, ecology, and professional development, with a strong emphasis on youth engagement. Through collaboration with local and international partners, "PLAMEN" aims to promote democratic values, mental health awareness, encourage social entrepreneurship and enhance youth mobility. We use non-formal education to advocate for environmental sustainability and empower youth trough Erasmus+ youth exchanges and training courses..
